### Mystock
* * *
一个实时的股票安卓应用程序,欢迎扫码体验：  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/appdebug.png">  

目前支持以下功能：  
1.生成数据，抓取A股sina股票数据保存本地数据库  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/data.PNG" height="450">  

2.实时更新股票数据  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/index.PNG" height="450">  

3.注册、登陆功能，用户数据保存本地数据库  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/register.PNG" height="450"> <img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/login.PNG" height="450">  

4.查看我的股票盈利情况，和上证、深证、恒生、纳斯达克等主要指数对比  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/my.PNG" height="450">  

5.搜索抓取的股票数据，可添加、置顶、取消置顶自选的股票  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/search.PNG" height="450"> <img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/top.PNG" height="450">  

6.点击某只股票可查看股票分时、日K、周K、月K图  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/detail.PNG" height="450">  

7.买入、卖出等模拟交易操作。  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/buy.PNG" height="450"><img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/sell.PNG" height="450">  

8.查看持仓、历史等股票数据。  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/hold.PNG" height="450"> <img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/history.PNG" height="450">  

9.微信风格的图片查看操作，可以设置本地图片和网络图片作为头像，网络图片抓取百度数据。  

<img src="https://github.com/ChandlerZeng/MyStock/blob/master/images/photo.JPG" height="450">
